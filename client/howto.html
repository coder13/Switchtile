How to Play

Accounts:

The login button also acts a sign up button. Just type in your username and password and play.
If you don't create an account, your times will be saved locally. If you do make an account, your times will be saved on a server and you can participate in the leaderboards.


The game:

You start with the solved state, and clicking scramble or pressing space will scramble. If you click shift+space, it will scramble but won't time.
There are 2 different control schemes: either clicking and draging the tiles, or hovering over the tile with your mouse and using the WASD keys to move it.
